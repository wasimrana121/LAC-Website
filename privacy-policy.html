Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 8, 2026. This policy describes how LA Consulting Corporation collects, uses, and protects your personal information.

1. Who We Are

LA Consulting Corporation ("LA Consulting Corporation", "we", "us", or "our") is a consulting firm headquartered at 31-33 Hillhouse Ave, 3rd Floor, Bridgeport, CT 06606, USA. Our website is laconsultingcorp.com.

We provide AI strategy, workflow automation, and executive thought leadership services to businesses worldwide.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information you voluntarily provide to us, including:

  • Name, work email address, and company name when you contact us via our website form
  • Phone number and messaging data when you contact us via WhatsApp
  • Any additional information you choose to include in your messages or inquiries

We may also collect standard website analytics data (pages visited, browser type, device type, geographic region) through privacy-respecting analytics tools. We do not use invasive tracking or sell your data to advertisers.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and schedule discovery calls
  • Deliver the consulting services you have engaged us for
  • Send relevant service updates or follow-up communications
  • Improve our website and service offerings
  • Comply with legal obligations

We will never sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

4. SMS / Text Message Consent

If you provide your phone number via our contact form or WhatsApp and consent to receive text messages, you agree to receive SMS communications from LA Consulting Corporation related to your inquiry and our services. Message and data rates may apply. You may opt out at any time by replying STOP.

5. Data Sharing

We do not share your personal data with third parties except in the following circumstances:

  • Service delivery: We may share data with trusted subcontractors or affiliated entities (such as LA Consulting SMC-Private in Pakistan) solely to deliver the services you've engaged us for, under strict confidentiality agreements.
  • Legal compliance: We may disclose information if required to do so by law or in response to valid legal requests.
  • Business transfer: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of company assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These include secure communications protocols, access controls, and regular security reviews.

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, or as required by law. For general inquiries, we retain contact information for up to 24 months. For active client engagements, we retain project-related data for up to 7 years for legal and accounting purposes.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Object to or restrict certain processing of your data
  • Data portability (where applicable)

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at info@laconsultingcorp.com. We will respond within 30 days.

9. Cookies

Our website may use essential cookies to ensure proper functionality. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies. If we introduce analytics tools in the future, we will update this policy and provide appropriate consent mechanisms.

10. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites (such as LinkedIn, WhatsApp, or Facebook). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before sharing any personal information with them.

11. Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for business professionals and are not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed.

13. Contact Us

If you have questions, complaints, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:
